Anne Wheeler

played by Zendaya. Anne is an introverted, audacious and self-conscious African American woman. She is a confident and experienced trapeze artist, having performed with her brother for many years. Throughout the movie Anne is seen to be deeply affected by the racist times of the 19th Century. Her internal conflicts, partnered with her skin color discrimination, stop her from feeling truly accepted in society.
